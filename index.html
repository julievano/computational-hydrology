Welcome to the Computational Hydrology Website

This site contains an integrated collection of tools to support future water resources planning and management. This includes advances in hydrometeorological datasets, climate downscaling, hydrologic modeling, climate impact assessements, and streamflow prediction, packaged as open-source software, freely available datasets, and guidance on appropriate use. Collectively these tools provide a foundation for understanding and adapting to future change. This website is dedicated to explaining each tool and how they fit together, documenting for both the science and applications communities resources that are currently available and major activities currently underway.
